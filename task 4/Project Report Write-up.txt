The Impact of Digital Professionalism

During my time as a Student Digital Ambassador, I had to compile a comprehensive guide on safety in social media and digital communication. This experience showed me how important it is to bridge the gap between casual online behavior and professional conduct.

I saw this gap in action when a student contacted a lecturer to discuss their grade appeal. The student’s casual language, such as using "Hey," "u," and "nvm," and not including a subject line in their email, ensured their appeal was not taken seriously. This lack of professionalism meant they did not get their grade appeal heard.

Key Takeaways:
Clarity is Key: A well-structured email with a subject line and greeting will get the message across.
Digital Footprint: This is not just limited to emails but also affects our online presence.
Ambassador Goal: My aim as a Student Digital Ambassador is to make students understand how their online behavior, whether in an email or on social media, affects their lives in the real world.
