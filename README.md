## 📝 Project Overview
This repository serves as my **Digital Literacy Portfolio** in my capacity as a **Student Digital Ambassador**. The primary goal of this initiative is to empower peers to navigate professional online spaces effectively and communicate safely within the evolving digital ecosystem.

This portfolio documents the completion of five mandatory modules designed to build a robust digital foundation.

---

## 📂 Portfolio Modules

### 🔹 Task 1: Digital Awareness
* **Objective:** Visualizing the core pillars of digital literacy.
* **Deliverable:** A comprehensive visual infographic created via **Canva/Prezi** highlighting essential digital skills and awareness.

### 🔹 Task 2: Digital Portfolio
* **Objective:** Establishing a professional digital footprint.
* **Deliverable:** Optimized professional profiles on **GitHub** and **LinkedIn** to showcase academic and technical growth.

### 🔹 Task 3: Platforms & Engagement
* **Objective:** Interactive learning and skill assessment.
* **Deliverable:** * Completion of a beginner-level coding challenge.
    * A **Google Form-based** 'Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz' designed for peer engagement.

### 🔹 Task 4: Professional Communication
* **Objective:** Mastering digital etiquette and safe interaction.
* **Deliverable:** * Professional email drafts for academic and corporate environments.
    * A Social Media **"Do's and Don'ts"** guide for maintaining a clean online reputation.

### 🔹 Task 5: Cybercrime & Safety
* **Objective:** Understanding threats and implementing prevention.
* **Deliverable:** * An in-depth **Case Study** on modern cyber threats.
    * A **'Stay Safe Online'** prevention guide focusing on data privacy and security best practices.
